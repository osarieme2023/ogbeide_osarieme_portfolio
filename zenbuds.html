Project Details

Zenbuds

IDP 3 Assignment

September-December 2024

Team

Ogbeide Osarieme

Role

3D Artist/Animator

Video Editor

Duration

14 Weeks

ZenBuds are a unique and stylish pair of earbuds designed to deliver an immersive audio experience
while promoting relaxation and mindfulness. Created from scratch, these earbuds feature a sleek design
and high-quality sound, making them perfect for listening to music, podcasts, or guided meditations.
With their comfortable fit and intuitive controls, ZenBuds are ideal for anyone looking to enhance their
daily routine with soothing audio experiences.

The Challenge

I struggled to accurately model the intricate details and achieve a realistic texture for the
ZenBuds in Cinema 4D, requiring several iterations to perfect the design.

The Solution

To overcome this, I refined my modeling techniques by studying advanced tutorials and
experimenting with different texturing methods in Cinema 4D, ultimately achieving the desired
realistic look for the ZenBuds.

The Outcome

The final ZenBuds 3D model accurately captured the product's sleek design and intricate details,
resulting in a visually stunning representation that effectively showcased the earbuds' innovative
features and aesthetic appeal.
